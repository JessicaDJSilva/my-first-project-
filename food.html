Brazil Cuisine

Brazilian cuisine is a melting pot of different nations dishes, comprising of European, Native, African, and Japanese influences. This combination comes about due to the rich mixed history of Brazil. Lots of unique and delicious dishes can be found in Brazilian restaurants and bakeries, and the variety is heightened even further across the different regions in Brazil, with each state having a distinct culinary identity.

Tradicional dishes-and-drinks

Brazilian Feijoada is a black bean and pork stew that is often served with farofa, toasted cassava flour. This comfort food is sometimes called the national dish of Brazil.

Pão de queijo (pronounced pow-ge-kay-ju) is a small golden cheese balls with a crunchy crust, a light, fluffy centre and a slightly tart flavour. They are similar to French gougère but are naturally gluten free. traditional of the state of minas gerais but loved by all the country.

Pastel is a delicious fried pastry the filling can be sweet or savoury it is a adaptation of the chinise fried wonton, it's very commoum on Sao Paulo open markets and normally served with sugarcane juice.

Brazilian Barbecue is a must try, the delicious variety of meat and the rich flavours make it irresistible on the photo it's rump steak called picanha without a doubt the favorite meat on the barbecue.

Moqueca is a Brazilian seafood stew. It is slowly cooked in a terracotta cassole. Moqueca can be made with prawn or fish as a base with tomatoes, onions, garlic, lime and coriander.It a dish typical of northern Brazil

Prawn on the pumpkin is very unique, its have prawn on a coconut milk and double cream sauce serverd on a roast pumpkin for who like seafood its is definitively worth trying.

Coxinha is very popular food in Brazil consisting of chopped or shredded chicken meat, covered in dough, molded into a shape resembling a teardrop, battered and fried.Coxinha is a popular food in Brazil consisting of chopped or shredded chicken meat, covered in dough, molded into a shape resembling a teardrop, battered and fried.

The national drink in Brazil caipirinha is made with cachaça (sugarcane hard liquor), sugar, and lime. The drink is prepared by mixing the fruit and the sugar together, then adding the liquor.

Desserts.

Tapioca is a starch extracted from the storage roots of the cassava plant.It's a kind of pancakes is normally served with sweet filling but the neutral flavor allow you to also try with savoury filling like cheese.

Pudding of condensed milk it's classic dessert with very simple Ingredients just need,sugar,condensed milk, eggs and whole milk, the secret of a good Pudding blend it for long time to get rid of the eggy smell.

Brigadeiro is very popular in birthday party It's a mix of milk and cocoa power making a soft ball of chocolate.

Beijinho (little kiss) is sibling of Brigadeiro but with coconut and white chocolate .

Romeo and Juliet is guava jelly with minas cheese very unique and excellent comination.

Corn Pudding is Tradicional on june and july in Brazil on the festival called "festa junina" is the month where corn is harvest so most of the dish of the festival is base on corn.
