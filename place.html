Most Beautiful Places in Brazil

Brazil has a very diverse natural beauty and geography thanks to it's large size and diverse cultural identity. For example:

Maragogi on state of Alagoas

Campos do Jordão on the state of São Paulo

Iguazú Falls or Iguaçu Falls are waterfalls of the Iguazu River on state of Paraná

The Catimbau National Park is a national park in the state of Pernambuco

Gramado is a mountain resort town in Brazil’s southernmost state, Rio Grande do Sul

Holambra is a municipality in the state of São Paulo in Brazil

Pelourinho is a city in state of Bahia.

Pipa has one of the most famous beaches of Brazil in capital city of state of Rio Grande do Norte.
